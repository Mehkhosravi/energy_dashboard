Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

Sustainable Energy Platform (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

1. What Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum necessary information to provide access to our energy dashboard:

  • Username
  • Email address (used for account verification and communication)
  • Password (securely stored using hashing)

2. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate and secure access to the dashboard
  • Communicate essential information (e.g., password resets)

Our lawful basis for processing your data is:

  • Contractual necessity – to provide the service you've registered for
  • Consent – for email communications, if applicable

3. Data Storage and Security

Your password is encrypted using secure hashing. We apply technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or loss.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data. We may disclose it only:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To service providers under confidentiality agreements, only if necessary

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use only essential session cookies for authentication. No third-party tracking or analytics cookies are used.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights Under the GDPR

You have the following rights:

  • Right to Access – request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification – correct any incorrect or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure – request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing – request limited use of your data
  • Right to Data Portability – receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object – object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@sustainableEnergyPlatform.com

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available on this page with the latest effective date.
